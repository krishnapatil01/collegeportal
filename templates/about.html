🏫 About Our Portal

Connecting Students, Alumni and Careers

Trinity Academy Alumni Portal is designed to build a strong academic and professional network where students and alumni can connect, share opportunities, and grow together.

Who We Are

About Trinity Alumni Portal

A digital platform created to strengthen relationships between students, alumni, and the institution through career support, mentorship, resources, job opportunities, and communication.

🎯

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students with direct access to alumni guidance, job updates, internship opportunities, resume analysis, academic resources, and career support. The portal helps students prepare for professional success with meaningful connections.

🚀

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a smart, connected, and supportive alumni ecosystem where every student receives guidance, every alumni can contribute, and every opportunity can reach the right candidate at the right time.

Portal Features

What This Portal Provides

The portal brings multiple student and alumni services into one professional platform.

👥

Alumni Search

Students can search alumni by company, location, profile, and professional details to build useful career connections.

💼

Jobs & Internships

Alumni and admins can share job and internship posts to help students find better career opportunities.

📄

Resume Analyzer

Students can upload resumes and receive parsed skills, education details, experience details, and job recommendations.

🤖

Career Chatbot

Chatbot support helps users with resume upload, jobs, education guidance, alumni search, notes, login, and portal navigation.

📚

Notes & Resources

Students can access useful academic notes, study materials, and resources uploaded by alumni or authorized users.

🔐

Secure Access

Admin approval and secure login help manage student and alumni accounts in a safe and organized way.
